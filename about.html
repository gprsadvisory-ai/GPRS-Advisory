GPRS Advisory is a boutique M&A advisory firm focused on the transactions that matter most — strategic sales, ownership transitions and capital partnerships for founder-led and privately held businesses. Our role is singular: identify value, maintain process discipline and maximise outcome. Not through volume or broad market exposure — but through preparation rigour, curated relationships and controlled execution from first engagement to completion. We bring the discipline of institutional M&A to the segment where it has the greatest impact — and where most value is won or lost. The GPRS Advisory Standard When a transaction is run properly, the outcome speaks for itself. Founders achieve the value their business deserves. Capital accesses opportunities that are prepared, positioned and ready to transact. That standard does not change by engagement, by mandate size or by complexity. It is the only way we work. The Problem We Solve Most transactions don't fail. They underperform. Three variables determine whether a transaction delivers its potential. Most processes get all three wrong. Preparation Underprepared for market Businesses reach market without financial normalisation, strategic positioning or risk identification. Buyers discount immediately — and never revise upward. Buyer Alignment Wrong counterparties engaged No institutional capital. No competitive tension. The seller ends up negotiating against themselves with buyers who were never the right fit. Process Control No structure, no leverage Without structured timelines and bid governance, buyers set the pace. Timelines extend. Prices erode. Deals collapse at the final stage. The cost is not a failed transaction. It is a transaction that closes — at the wrong price, on the wrong terms, after months of unnecessary attrition. Our Market One Market. Absolute Focus. The mid-market is not a secondary focus for GPRS Advisory — it is our entire focus. Our buyer relationships, process frameworks, positioning discipline and advisory capabilities are all built and calibrated for this segment. We do not handle business brokerage. We do not advise on institutional transactions. We operate in one segment, at a high standard, with genuine commitment to outcome on every engagement.
