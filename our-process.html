Our Process

Value is created before and during the process — not at the end. Every GPRS Advisory engagement follows a disciplined four-phase framework designed to maximise outcome, maintain confidentiality and deliver certainty of completion.

Every GPRS Advisory engagement follows a disciplined four-phase framework. Value is created before and during the process — not at the end.

Phase 01

Strategic Assessment
& Readiness

Before the first buyer sees the business, we do the work that protects its value — financial normalisation, risk identification, and strategic positioning.

Phase 02

Market
Preparation

We build the equity story, information memorandum and financial pack that give sophisticated counterparties the confidence — and information — to move decisively.

Phase 03

Engagement
& Offers

Competitive tension is engineered, not hoped for. We run a structured bid process that keeps qualified buyers moving simultaneously — creating upward price pressure.

Phase 04

Execution
& Completion

Senior attention intensifies as the process approaches completion. We coordinate due diligence, manage issues and drive documentation through to final settlement.
