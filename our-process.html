Phase 01 Strategic Assessment & Readiness Before the first buyer sees the business, we do the work that protects its value — financial normalisation, risk identification, and strategic positioning. Phase 02 Market Preparation We build the equity story, information memorandum and financial pack that give sophisticated counterparties the confidence — and information — to move decisively. Phase 03 Engagement & Offers Competitive tension is engineered, not hoped for. We run a structured bid process that keeps qualified buyers moving simultaneously — creating upward price pressure. Phase 04 Execution & Completion Senior attention intensifies as the process approaches completion. We coordinate due diligence, manage issues and drive documentation through to final settlement.
